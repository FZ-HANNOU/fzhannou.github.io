About Me

Hello! I'm Dr. Fatma-Zohra Hannou Research scientist working on Artificial Intelligence topics. In addition to knowledge graphs, interoperability and languague processing, I study interesting use cases where combining symbolic and neuronal systems might push current AI limits.
